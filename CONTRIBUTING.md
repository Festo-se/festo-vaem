# Contributing to festo-vaem

Thank you for your interest in contributing to festo-vaem! We are a lean team and are grateful for contributions from the community. 

Did you find a bug?
- If you have found a security-related bug, please do NOT use the Github Issue tracker to submit. Instead, please refer to the reporting procedure outlined in our Security guidelines found [here](SECURITY.md). 

- Before reporting non-security bugs, please search the [issue tracker](https://github.com/Festo-se/festo-vaem/issues) to see if your issue has already been reported. 

- If your bug has not been reported, please [open a new issue](https://github.com/Festo-se/festo-vaem/issues/new). Include a title, a clear and concise summary of the bugged behavior, as much contextual information for your operational set-up as possible, and code examples demonstrating the bug. 

## New Contributors

Want to contribute to the codebase itself? Please get started at the README to set up your clone of our repo, make a fork, and make a pull request from your fork with your changes for us to review!

- [Setting up Git](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/git-basics/set-up-git) 

- [Pull requests](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests)

## Restrictions on Generative AI Usage

Due to the increasing amount of automated, LLM-derived traffic, and due to the fact we are a small team, we must place the following restrictions on the usage of generative AI output:

1. Pull requests, issue creation, and comments on the repository shall not be generated using an LLM or generative AI. Such content presents a substantial impediment to content-ful and meaningful collaboration with our users. 

2. It is incumbent upon any contributing developer to understand **ALL** changes they contribute, whether it be to documentation, tests, or the codebase itself, whether or not they are generatively derived from an LLM or other source. Any contributing developer shall take great care when using generative tools to aid in their contributions and verify that they understand what they are contributing. 


We reserve the right to summarily decline any pull request and close any issue that does not meet these requirements. Repeat offenders will be warned and then banned from discussions and contributions.
